This is a math question I got on a test before
I still remember it because the solution is pretty cool.

A circle is drawn on a page. First, you draw a straight line that goes through the center of the circle from one side to the other. Then you draw a second straight line, also going through the center, but its angle is chosen at random. What is the chance that the two lines cross each other inside the circle?

The answer should be in fraction form.
For example, if you believe the answer is 15% then that is 3/20
